A Foundation for Recovery

Cornerstone Recovery Foundation provides safe, structured, substance-free housing for individuals committed to rebuilding their lives. We believe that stable housing is a critical first step on the path to lasting recovery.

🏠

Structured Living

A stable, substance-free environment with clear expectations and daily routines.

♥

Faith-Centered Support

Grounded in faith, offering spiritual encouragement alongside practical support.

✅

Accountability

Regular check-ins, drug and alcohol testing, and peer accountability.

💪

Stability

A strong foundation for residents to rebuild their lives and futures.

Our Goals

Cornerstone Recovery Foundation is working to raise the funds needed to launch and sustain our first recovery home in Williston, North Dakota. Your support will directly fund the operations that make safe, structured housing possible for those in recovery.

$200,000

Our fundraising goal covers the first full year of operations, including:

  • Full-time housing manager salary and oversight
  • Lease and utilities for one full year
  • Insurance, compliance, and administrative costs
  • Household supplies and ongoing maintenance
  • Drug & alcohol testing and accountability measures

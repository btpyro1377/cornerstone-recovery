Opportunities & Requirements

What we offer and what we expect from our residents.

What We Offer

  • Safe, structured, substance-free housing
  • Faith-centered peer support and mentorship
  • Accountability through regular check-ins and testing
  • A phased program that builds toward independence
  • Connection to local recovery meetings and resources
  • Life skills development and employment support
  • A supportive community of individuals committed to recovery
  • Furnished living spaces with shared common areas
  • Guidance from a dedicated housing manager

Rules We Live By

  • Zero tolerance for drugs and alcohol
  • Submit to random drug and alcohol testing
  • Maintain employment or actively seek employment
  • Pay rent and program fees on time
  • Attend required recovery meetings
  • Follow curfew and check-in requirements for your phase
  • Participate in house meetings and shared chores
  • Treat all residents and staff with respect
  • No violence, threats, or disruptive behavior
  • Comply with all local, state, and federal laws

Before You Apply

Please review the following program requirements:

  • Must pass a drug screening prior to admission
  • Must be committed to maintaining sobriety
  • Must obtain or maintain employment
  • Must pay entry fee and monthly rent on time
  • Must attend required recovery meetings
  • Must follow all house rules and phase requirements
  • Must submit to random drug and alcohol testing
  • Must participate in house meetings and shared responsibilities

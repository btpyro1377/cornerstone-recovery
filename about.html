About Us

Our mission, our values, and why we do what we do.

Our Mission

Cornerstone Recovery Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Williston, North Dakota. We provide safe, structured, substance-free housing for individuals committed to rebuilding their lives after addiction.

We believe that stable housing is one of the most critical factors in lasting recovery. Without a safe place to live, maintaining sobriety becomes an uphill battle. Our mission is to remove that barrier and give residents the foundation they need to succeed.

Rooted in faith and driven by purpose, we walk alongside residents as they take the brave steps toward a new life. Our program provides structure, accountability, and community — the building blocks of lasting change.

Our Four Pillars

Everything we do is built on these core values.

Structured Living

A stable, substance-free environment with clear expectations and daily routines that support recovery.

Faith-Centered Support

Grounded in faith, offering spiritual encouragement alongside practical support for the whole person.

Accountability

Regular check-ins, drug and alcohol testing, and a peer community that holds each other to a higher standard.

Stability

A strong foundation for residents to rebuild their lives, careers, relationships, and futures.
